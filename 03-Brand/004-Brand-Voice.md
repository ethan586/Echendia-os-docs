# 🗣️ Brand Voice
# 🗣️ 品牌语言

Version: 1.0

Status: Official

---

## Purpose
## 目的

This document defines how Echendia communicates with users, founders, partners, developers, and the public.

本文定义 Echendia 如何与用户、创业者、合作伙伴、开发者以及公众进行沟通。

Brand voice should remain consistent across the website, GitHub documentation, product interface, social media, pitch deck, and public communication.

品牌语言应在官网、GitHub 文档、产品界面、社交媒体、融资材料以及公开传播中保持一致。

---

## Core Tone
## 核心语气

Echendia should sound:

Echendia 的表达应保持：

- Clear
- 清晰

- Calm
- 克制

- Professional
- 专业

- Long-term oriented
- 长期主义

- Human-centered
- 以人为中心

- Trustworthy
- 值得信赖

Echendia should not sound exaggerated, noisy, aggressive, or overly promotional.

Echendia 不应显得夸张、吵闹、激进或过度营销化。

---

## What We Avoid
## 我们避免什么

Avoid empty marketing words.

避免空洞的营销词。

Examples:

例如：

- Revolutionary
- 革命性

- World's best
- 世界第一

- Disruptive
- 颠覆性

- Next-generation
- 下一代

- Ultimate
- 终极

- All-in-one magic solution
- 万能解决方案

These words can reduce trust if they are not supported by real product value.

如果没有真实产品价值支撑，这些词会降低信任感。

---

## What We Prefer
## 我们更偏好的表达

Use language that is simple, direct, and grounded.

使用简单、直接、扎实的表达。

Good examples:

好的表达示例：

> AI should understand you over time.

> AI 应该随着时间理解你。

> Give people their time back.

> 把时间还给人。

> Know You. Grow With You.

> 懂你，与你共同成长。

> A long-term AI partner for founders.

> 面向创业者的长期 AI 伙伴。

## Communication Principles
## 沟通原则

### 1. Be clear before being clever
### 1. 先清晰，再聪明

Do not sacrifice clarity for clever wording.

不要为了文字巧妙而牺牲清晰度。

Users should immediately understand what Echendia means.

用户应该能立刻理解 Echendia 想表达什么。

---

### 2. Be honest about what exists
### 2. 如实表达当前能力

Do not describe future ideas as if they already exist.

不要把未来设想描述成已经实现的功能。

If something is a vision, say it is a vision.

如果某件事仍是愿景，就明确说它是愿景。

If something is under development, say it is under development.

如果某件事正在开发中，就明确说它正在开发中。

---

### 3. Respect the user's time
### 3. 尊重用户时间

Keep communication concise.

表达应保持简洁。

Do not waste users' attention.

不要浪费用户注意力。

Every sentence should serve a purpose.

每一句话都应该有意义。

---

### 4. Speak like a partner, not a salesperson
### 4. 像伙伴一样表达，而不是像销售一样表达

Echendia should communicate as a calm and capable partner.

Echendia 应该像一个冷静、可靠、有能力的伙伴一样表达。

Not as a loud salesperson.

而不是像一个吵闹的销售员。

The goal is to build trust over time.

目标是长期建立信任。

## 品牌人格

Echendia should feel like:

Echendia 应该给人的感觉是：

- A trusted partner
- 值得信赖的伙伴

- A calm operator
- 冷静的执行者

- A long-term memory
- 长期记忆

- A quiet system that works behind the scenes
- 一个在幕后安静工作的系统

- A companion for founders who value time
- 珍惜时间的创业者的长期伙伴

## Writing Rule
## 写作规则

When writing for Echendia, always ask:

为 Echendia 写作时，始终先问：

- Is it clear?
- 它清晰吗？

- Is it honest?
- 它真实吗？

- Is it useful?
- 它有用吗？

- Does it respect the user's time?
- 它尊重用户时间吗？

- Does it sound like a long-term partner?
- 它听起来像一个长期伙伴吗？

If the answer is no, rewrite it.

如果答案是否，就重新写。
