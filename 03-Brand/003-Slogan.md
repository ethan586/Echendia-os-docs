# 🌟 Slogan
# 🌟 品牌标语

Version: 1.0

Status: Official

---

## Official Brand Slogan
## 官方品牌标语

**Know You. Grow With You.**

**懂你，与你共同成长。**

---

## Meaning
## 含义

Echendia is not designed to be another temporary AI assistant.

Echendia 并不是为了成为另一个临时性的 AI 助手。

It is designed to become a long-term AI partner that continuously understands its users and grows together with them.

它的目标是成为一个长期 AI 伙伴，持续理解用户，并与用户共同成长。

The value of Echendia does not only come from intelligence.

Echendia 的价值不只来自智能。

Its deeper value comes from long-term understanding, memory, and companionship.

它更深层的价值，来自长期理解、长期记忆以及长期陪伴。

---

## Brand Interpretation
## 品牌解读

**Know You** means Echendia should understand the user's work, life, goals, habits, context, and long-term needs.

**懂你** 意味着 Echendia 应该理解用户的工作、生活、目标、习惯、上下文以及长期需求。

**Grow With You** means Echendia should become more useful over time as it learns more about the user.

**与你共同成长** 意味着随着 Echendia 越来越了解用户，它也应该随着时间变得越来越有价值。

---

## Usage
## 使用规范

The official English slogan should be used as:

**Know You. Grow With You.**

官方英文标语统一使用：

**Know You. Grow With You.**

The official Chinese slogan should be used as:

**懂你，与你共同成长。**

官方中文标语统一使用：

**懂你，与你共同成长。**

Do not frequently modify this slogan.

不要频繁修改该品牌标语。

It should be used consistently across the website, GitHub documentation, pitch deck, product pages, and public communication.

它应统一用于官网、GitHub 文档、融资材料、产品页面以及对外传播内容中。
