**Know You. Grow With You.**

## 🚀 Our Vision
## 🚀 我们的愿景

Echendia OS aims to become the AI Operating System for founders.

Echendia OS 的目标，是成为面向创业者的 AI 操作系统。

We believe AI should not only answer questions.

我们相信，AI 不应该只是回答问题。

AI should remember, understand, execute, and grow with its user.

AI 应该能够记住用户、理解用户、执行任务，并与用户共同成长。

---

## 🧠 Long-Term Memory
## 🧠 长期记忆

Today’s AI often forgets users after each conversation.

今天的大多数 AI，往往会在一次对话结束后忘记用户。

Echendia OS believes human life is continuous, so AI should be continuous too.

Echendia OS 相信，人的人生是连续的，所以 AI 也应该是连续的。

The more AI knows about a founder’s work, life, habits, health, goals, and business, the more valuable it becomes.

AI 越了解创业者的工作、生活、习惯、健康、目标和公司，就越能创造价值。

---

## 🤖 AI With Action
## 🤖 具备执行能力的 AI

Echendia OS is not just an AI chat interface.

Echendia OS 不只是一个 AI 聊天界面。

It is designed to connect with tools, databases, workflows, and the MCP ecosystem.

它将连接工具、数据库、工作流以及 MCP 生态。

AI should become the user’s digital brain, long-term memory, and hands.

AI 应该成为用户的数字大脑、长期记忆和双手。

---

## ⏳ Time
## ⏳ 时间

The ultimate goal of Echendia OS is to give founders their time back.

Echendia OS 的最终目标，是把时间还给创业者。

AI should reduce repetitive work, lower cognitive load, improve quality of life, and help founders focus on creation, decision-making, and meaningful life.

AI 应该减少重复劳动，降低认知负担，提高生活质量，让创业者把精力放在创造、决策和有意义的生活上。

## 🌍 Long-Term Future
## 🌍 长期未来

In five years, we hope every founder will use an AI Operating System like Echendia OS.

五年以后，我们希望每一位创业者都会使用像 Echendia OS 这样的 AI 操作系统。

Our long-term vision is simple:

我们的长期愿景很简单：

> Every founder deserves an AI that never stops learning them.

> 每一位创业者，都值得拥有一个永远不会停止了解自己的 AI。
