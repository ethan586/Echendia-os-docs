# 🌟 Our Core Values
# 🌟 我们的核心价值观

These values define how Echendia OS is designed, developed, and evolved.

这些价值观定义了 Echendia OS 如何设计、如何开发以及如何持续演进。

Every future feature, AI Agent, workflow, and product decision should follow these principles.

未来所有功能、AI Agent、工作流以及产品决策，都应遵循这些原则。

---

# 1️⃣ Understand First
# 1️⃣ 理解优先

Before AI can help people, it must first understand them.

在 AI 帮助用户之前，它必须先理解用户。

Understanding is more valuable than simply answering.

真正的理解，比简单回答问题更有价值。

---

# 2️⃣ Remember Continuously
# 2️⃣ 持续记忆

Human life is continuous.

People should not need to repeat themselves every day.

人的人生是连续的。

用户不应该每天重复介绍自己。

AI should build long-term memory and continuously learn about its users.

AI 应该建立长期记忆，并持续了解用户。

---

# 3️⃣ Execute Reliably
# 3️⃣ 可靠执行

AI should not stop at giving advice.

It should help users complete real work safely and reliably.

AI 不应停留在提供建议。

它应该安全、可靠地帮助用户完成真正的工作。

Execution creates value.

执行才能真正创造价值。

---

# 4️⃣ Grow Together
# 4️⃣ 共同成长

The relationship between AI and people should become stronger over time.

AI 与人的关系应该随着时间不断成长。

The more AI understands its users, the more valuable it becomes.

AI 越了解用户，就越有价值。

We believe AI should grow together with its users.

我们相信 AI 应该与用户共同成长。

---

# ❤️ Our Promise
# ❤️ 我们的承诺

Every decision we make should make people's lives simpler, not more complicated.

我们的每一个产品决策，都应该让生活变得更简单，而不是更复杂。

Every feature should help people get their time back.

每一个功能，都应该帮助用户把时间重新拿回来。

---

# 🌍 Our Belief
# 🌍 我们的信念

Technology should adapt to people.

People should not adapt to technology.

技术应该适应人。

而不是让人去适应技术。

This belief will guide Echendia OS for years to come.

这一信念将长期指导 Echendia OS 的发展。
