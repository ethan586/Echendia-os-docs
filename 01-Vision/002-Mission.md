# 🎯 Our Mission
# 🎯 我们的使命

**We exist to give people their time back.**

**我们的存在，是为了把时间还给人。**

---

# 🌍 Why We Exist
# 🌍 我们为什么存在

Time is the most valuable and non-renewable resource in life.

时间，是人生中最宝贵且无法再生的资源。

People should spend their time creating, thinking, learning, building meaningful relationships, and enjoying life.

人们应该把时间花在创造、思考、学习、建立有意义的人际关系以及享受生活上。

Instead, many founders spend countless hours on repetitive work, fragmented tools, endless notifications, and unnecessary complexity.

然而，现实中许多创业者每天却花费大量时间处理重复劳动、切换各种软件、应对无数通知以及复杂的工作流程。

We believe AI should handle those repetitive tasks.

我们相信，这些重复性的工作应该交给 AI。

---

# 🤖 Our Responsibility
# 🤖 我们的责任

Echendia OS is designed to become a trusted AI Operating System that continuously understands its users and helps them work more efficiently.

Echendia OS 致力于成为一个值得信赖的 AI 操作系统，不断理解用户，并帮助用户提升工作效率。

Our responsibility is not to replace people.

我们的责任不是取代人。

Our responsibility is to remove unnecessary work.

我们的责任是消除不必要的工作。

AI should become a long-term partner that remembers, understands, executes, and grows together with its users.

AI 应该成为一个长期伙伴，能够记住用户、理解用户、执行任务，并与用户共同成长。

---

# ❤️ What We Hope
# ❤️ 我们希望

We hope founders can spend less time managing software and more time building meaningful products and living meaningful lives.

我们希望创业者能够减少管理软件的时间，把更多时间投入到打造优秀产品以及享受有意义的人生中。

---

# 🌟 使命宣言

> Give people their time back.

> 把时间还给人。
